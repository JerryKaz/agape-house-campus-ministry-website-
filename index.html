About Our Ministry

Our History & Purpose

Founded in 2015, Agape House UPSA campus ministry provides a spiritual home for students, helping them grow in faith and character during their academic journey.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To see every UPSA student transformed by Christ's love and empowered to impact their world.

Mission: To disciple students through biblical teaching, authentic community, and practical service.

Core Values

Biblical Authority, Christ-Centred Worship, Authentic Community, Servant Leadership, and Excellence in All Things.

Spiritual Growth

We provide spaces for prayer, Bible study, mentoring, and fellowship to help students develop a vibrant, resilient faith that lasts beyond campus life.
